Overload would not exist without the mind of some 19-year old BMCC student who is currently majoring Animation and Motion Graphics.

A lover of animation, cartoons, and all sorts of things that move. I never really wasn't the type of person that thinks art is some sort of escapism, I think it's a world of bizarre ideas. I take ideas, and mash them together with whatever I want because originality is hard, but it won't stop any of us from striving for that is it? If there is a status quo...we all have the choice to break it, that is where OVERLOAD came from. Apply logic, then ignore it entirely!
